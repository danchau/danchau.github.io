Hawaii Trip [04/01/15]

"Here is the two-part series of my trip to Hawaii over the 2014 Summer Break. During the break, I set myself a goal to try out "cinematography". Honestly, it was a lot harder than I thought. Not that I thought it was going to be a cake walk. May I present you: shaky shots, sub-par color grading, songs that hardly fit the tone of the video, and excessive film burns. But one does not conquer the film world in 2 weeks. I will continue making videos because frankly, it's a f*cking blast. People say "pictures can mean a thousand words". I say "videos can mean a million words". Not that pictures are less superior but videos provide a whole different viewing experience. Well, that's it. More videos to come in the future. Hopefully better ones. Peace.
